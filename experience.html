MY EXPERIENCES



Sergeant Staff Appointment Camp for District Level Camp of Ikhtiar Hidup School Level Foot Parade
I acquired experience managing a large group and learnt to manage the obligations assigned to me as a leader during the camp. I got the opportunity to live a soldier's life and work with other members of my group to get by without the use of special equipment. It was my first time marching in front of 700 people as part of the Kokurilum Day celebrations at the school level.
I got the rank of Staff Sergeant for the District Level and had the opportunity to join the next camp to get the Rank of Officer Setting up a tent, cooking with a stove, and learning how to use a compass are just a few of the activities available. The goal of this activity is to help students enhance their mental and physical capabilities. For Co-Curriculum Day, the School Youth Cadet Uniformed Body (KRS) took second place in the best parade award.
